About Us
Grand It

On 1 November 2003, Grand IT Hypermarket Sdn Bhd (“Grand IT”) made a ground breaking news for being Malaysia's 1st and Largest IT Retail Outlet in a hypermarket concept. Since our soft opening on 2 April 2003, we recognized the needs for a more differentiated and dedicated IT outlet that not only provides the ease of shopping at leisure but also a better-organized outlet that appeals to every walk of life. To our delight, this new concept is much accepted and awaited by fellow Malaysians as evidenced in the rapid growth of our Company. Undoubtedly, we aspire to be No.1 in all areas of IT Retailing, not just to remain as a fading pioneer, by constantly reinventing new ideas to add value to our product ranges and services that we offer, from launching membership loyalty program to online purchase. We have certainly turned on the competitive fever among our rivals and the competitive landscape in the IT industry has never been the same again since our arrival.
