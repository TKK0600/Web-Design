About Us

Here you can find the frequently asked questions by our customers. If your questions are not listed here, please do not hesitate to contact us; we will try our very best to answer your questions as soon as possible. All contact informations are listed below this page.

What is Grand IT?
Grand IT is a retailer of only the best and highest-quality gaming peripherals. We sell branded gaming peripherals such as Razer, ROG, Logitech, and more!
Do you sell products which are not shown on the website?
We only sell products which are listed on our website. Products of other models and brands are not sold in our stores.
Can I purchase your products online?
Yes, you can. You also also surf our website to know more about our products.
Can I try the items before I purchase them?
You can try our products in our physical stores, depending on their availability.
Are the prices listed on your website the same as in-store?
Yes, the prices listed on our websites are final and are inclusive of tax. However, prices may change depending on the discounts listed.
What are your stores' operating hours?
All of our stores are open from 8.00 a.m. to 10 p.m. from Mondays to Sundays, excluding public holidays.
What payment methods can I use to purchase items?
We accept debit and credit cards in our online store while cash, debit and credit cards, Boost, Grabpay, ShopeePay, Touch 'n go eWallet, WeChat Pay and BigPay in all of our physical stores.
Does my purchased item come with a warranty?
Yes! All purchased products from our stores are subjected to a local manufacturer warranty.
Can I return or refund purchased products?
Yes, all products purchased within 2 weeks may be returned and refunded. But depending on the situation, our staff may reject returns or refunds. Such cases include but are not limited to:
  • Items with missing or damaged parts;
  • Items purchased from other suppliers;
  • Items which are disassembled, modified, neglected, etc.
How can I contact Grand IT?
You may contact us via Whatsapp, email and phone. Our contact information is listed at our support pages.
